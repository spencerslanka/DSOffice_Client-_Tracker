DS Office — Client Tracker

Internal client status tracking system

A simple web application built for the front office of the Pelmadulla Divisional Secretariat. It helps the front desk register client visits, lets each section officer (Land / Planning / Account / Administration / Registration / Field) track their own queue, and shows a live public display of client progress in the waiting area.
